Experience the power of AI in our help desk system

About the AI Assistant

Our AI Assistant is powered by Google's Gemini technology, providing intelligent responses to help desk queries. It can explain technical concepts, suggest solutions, and assist both users and administrators.

Note: To use the AI Assistant, you need to set up a Google Gemini API key in your environment variables.

How AI Works in Our System

Ticket Summarization

The AI can automatically summarize lengthy ticket descriptions and histories into concise, actionable summaries for administrators.

Smart Reply Suggestions

When responding to tickets, the AI can suggest professional, context-aware replies to help administrators respond more efficiently.

Issue Categorization

The AI can analyze user descriptions and automatically categorize tickets into the most appropriate department and issue type.

Research Assistance

For complex issues, the AI can research potential solutions using Google Search and provide administrators with relevant troubleshooting steps.

Getting Started

  1. Obtain a Google Gemini API key from Google AI Studio
  2. Add the API key to your environment variables as API_KEY
  3. Restart the application to enable AI features
  4. Access AI features through the chatbot or admin ticket management interface
